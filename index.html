About SmartDesk

Hardware features

Smartdesk provides you with useful hardware features as well as software features. Firstly, we created the main screen of our desk with Super Amoled, which protects your eyesight and has high image quality. Thanks to the Wi-Fi technology available in our warning panel, we can connect to Smartdesk with your mobile phone at any time when you are not at home, create notes, warnings, and remind us of your notes at any time. Because our goal is to improve your education and training quality and improve your performance, you can scan the book, document you want with the scanner technology located on the right side of the screen of Smartdesk and instantly view the files you have scanned on Smartdesk. One of the most important things for us is that you are physically comfortable and relaxed. With the Smartdesk screen and the motion mechanism between the legs, you can move your foot up and down according to your own comfort and make it easier to operate. One of the biggest problems in Smartdesk, where so many technologies come together, is sensitivity and easy damage. For this reason, we created our entire desktop screen with the Corning Gorilla Grass 5, which is very resistant to water, bumps and we tried to minimize any potential damage. Besides these, we have produced an optional Smartable besides Smartdesk. Totally optional, Smartable offers you a more convenient and practical environment. The wheels are designed to move easily and in our design Smartable upper right hand side of the Smartdesk’s is placed on top of the Smartdesk to protect your Smartdesk against putting up your food and drinks while pouring or falling. With the smartphone integration in the first section, you can charge your phone quickly with the help of the phone case without using a cable with the charger feature. We designed our second section in a simple way away from the technology in order to store your items that you will use while working harder.

Technical features

To talk about SmartDesk's software details; First of all, we can talk about voice assistant.With this voice assistant, each user can define their own voice and create one interface.In addition, with the commands given by this voice assistant, you are doing a lot of your work in a short time, helping you by opening a separate window without leaving your work.This gives you time and speed. Another advantage is the mobile application. With the functions available in the mobile application, you can add notes to your desk and send messages at the same time.This is an important element for individuals to forget details because of the fact that today's life is fast.This is especially important for students.It is possible to remind the delivery of assigned assignments and access it conveniently on the analog screen to ensure the working order. You can modify the analog screen (notification screen) as you like, color it, make it fun, and make your work desk more interesting and attractive. The most important point is our operating system.You can perform various tasks by installing the desired work program in SmartDesk with the operating system.SmartDesk stands out as a very important technology for jobs that require visuals such as drawing programs, animation programs. And finally, with SmartDesk's play title, it gives you a comfortable working environment.With the included software, you can digitally angle the mask from 20 to 30 degrees horizontally, and adjust the height and maintain your work comfortably.
